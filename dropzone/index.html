Skydive Hibaldstow Staff

Join us at Skydive Hibaldstow

Our dropzone of choice is the highly regarded Skydive Hibaldstow situated in North Licolnshire approximately 50 miles from York. Equipped with some of the fastest Dornier G92 aircraft, Skydive Hibaldstow offers a bar serving hot food and drinks, an overnight bunkhouse with showers, free Wi-Fi and friendly staff.

Camping is free to those who can brave the cold, with the bunkhouse costing £5 per night per person (bring your own sleeping bag):

Accommodation:
Bed in shared room £5 per person per night
Standard bunkroom £20 per room
Bunkroom key deposit £20 per key

Travel information:

If travelling to and from Hibaldstow via train, connections are made via York > Doncaster > Scunthorpe costing approximately £15 for an off-peak open return with railcard and approximately £19 from Scunthorpe station to Hib via Taxi (20 minute journey) - so try and taxishare if you can!
