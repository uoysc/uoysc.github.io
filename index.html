Welcome to University of York Skydiving Club!

Skydiving is the highest adrenaline sport on offer here at York, and it's certainly not the kind of thing that you only want to do once! We offer a fun and friendly club that will help any student interested in all facets of the sport, introducing students to the great and incredibly inclusive community of people all united by their love of skydiving. Each weekend we organise transport to the local dropzone, so members can become fully qualified skydivers in no time. From this point on, the sky is literally the limit! Become a member today and take advantage of our great subsidies!
